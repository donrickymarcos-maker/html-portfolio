The Best Movies of All Time According to Don

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

1. Everything, Everywhere, All at Once

This made me cry. I hope to see something like this from the daniels again.

2. Parasite dir. Bong Joon-ho

This film is a social commentary on class struggle. It shows that even the poor can go against their own for the sake of pleasure.

3. Knives Out

This film is a brilliant example of the murder mystery genre that turns into something else entirely.
